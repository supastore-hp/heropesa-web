FAQ

What is salon software and what does it do?
Salon software helps beauty businesses manage appointments, payments, client communication, marketing, and inventory in one unified platform. Heropesa Appointments combines scheduling, POS, and business tools to streamline operations.
Which businesses should use salon software?
Any beauty or personal care business: hair salons, nail salons, barbershops, spas, tattoo studios, med spas, and freelance stylists. Heropesa scales from solo practitioners to multi-location enterprises.
Is Heropesa right for my business?
If you need online booking, payment processing, client management, or marketing tools, Heropesa is built for you. Start free and upgrade as you grow—no long-term contracts required.
How do I get started?
Sign up in minutes. Add your services, staff, and availability, then start accepting bookings immediately. Our team can help migrate your existing client data if needed.
How can I switch from my current booking solution?
Our onboarding specialists can help import your appointments, client lists, and service menus. Most businesses transition seamlessly within a few days.
How much will it cost to run my business with Heropesa?
Start free with Heropesa Free. Upgrade to Plus ($49/mo) or Premium ($149/mo) for advanced features. Payment processing fees apply per transaction. No hidden fees or long-term commitments.
