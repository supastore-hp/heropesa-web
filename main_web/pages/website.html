FAQ

Why did Heropesa Online change to websites?
Heropesa Online evolved into "Websites" to better reflect the expanded capabilities beyond just e-commerce, including portfolio sites, service booking, and custom domains, while keeping the same powerful integrated tools.
How do I start my website for free?
Sign up for a Heropesa account, select the Free plan, and use our drag-and-drop builder to choose a template, add products/services, and publish your site in minutes. No credit card required.
How to create an online store?
Log into your Heropesa Dashboard, navigate to Online Store, and follow the guided setup to add products, configure shipping/pickup, connect your domain, and launch your storefront.
Does Heropesa offer resources to help me build my site?
Yes. Heropesa provides step-by-step tutorials, design guides, SEO checklists, and 24/7 support documentation to help you build, optimize, and grow your online presence.
Can I connect my website to social media?
Absolutely. Heropesa integrates natively with Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and Google Shopping, allowing you to sync your catalog and sell directly across multiple social platforms.
Do I get free web hosting when I create a website with Heropesa?
Yes. All Heropesa Website plans include secure, high-speed cloud hosting with SSL encryption, automatic backups, and unlimited bandwidth at no extra cost.
