FAQ

What is a QSR POS system?
A Quick-Service Restaurant (QSR) POS is a point-of-sale system optimized for speed, high volume, and streamlined workflows. It handles fast order entry, integrated kitchen routing, payment processing, and inventory management specifically tailored for cafes, fast-casual, and takeout-focused businesses.
What's the difference between a QSR POS and a restaurant POS system?
While both handle orders and payments, a QSR POS prioritizes speed, self-ordering, combo building, and high-throughput reporting. A full-service restaurant POS focuses more on table management, coursing, tip tracking, and detailed guest profiling.
Can a QSR POS system handle online and mobile orders?
Yes. Heropesa seamlessly integrates web ordering, mobile apps, and third-party delivery marketplaces (like DoorDash and Uber Eats) directly into your main POS dashboard, keeping all orders, inventory, and sales data synchronized in real time.
