Have questions?

How do I run payroll myself?
Heropesa Payroll makes it easy to run payroll yourself directly from your Heropesa Dashboard. You can import timecards, review hours, and process payments with just a few clicks.
How do I run payroll processing for contractors?
With the Contractor-only plan, you can easily add 1099 contractors, enter their hours or flat payment amounts, and pay them through direct deposit or check while Heropesa generates the required tax forms.
What payroll tax filing forms do I need to submit for my business?
When you use Heropesa Full-Service Payroll, we automatically calculate, file, and pay your federal and state payroll taxes, including forms like W-2s, 1099s, and quarterly filings, so you don't have to submit them manually.
How do I pay multistate payroll taxes?
Heropesa Payroll supports multi-state operations by automatically handling the complex tax compliance and state-specific filings for employees living or working in different states.
How long does payroll take to process?
Processing takes just a few minutes. Depending on your setup and payment methods, funds can reach your team via next-day direct deposit or standard payment timelines.
