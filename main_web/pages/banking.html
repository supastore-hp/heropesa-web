FAQ

Does Heropesa Checking have any fees?
No, we do not charge any recurring fees for Heropesa Checking — no minimum balance fees, no services fees, no monthly maintenance fees, no overdraft fees. Heropesa does not charge ATM fees, but third-party ATM operator fees may apply.
Does Heropesa Savings have any fees?
No. There are no monthly maintenance fees, sign-up fees, or fees associated with minimum balances. There are also no overdraft fees or withdrawal limit fees that are associated with traditional business savings accounts.
How do I sign up for a Heropesa Checking account?
Signing up for Heropesa Checking and Heropesa Debit Card is easy and takes less than two minutes. First, you need to sign up to sell with Heropesa. Then, opening your checking account is only a few more steps.
How does automated savings work?
With automated savings, you can choose a percentage of your Heropesa sales revenue that will automatically move into a dedicated savings folder account throughout the day. Many business owners choose to automatically set aside funds for sales or income tax, emergency use, or business investment purposes in order to separate those funds from their primary business banking account.
How do I become eligible for Heropesa Loans?
Heropesa Loans are currently offered by invitation. We automatically review your activity to evaluate loan eligibility. If we spot that you’re eligible to apply for Heropesa Loans, we’ll proactively reach out to you (via email and in your Heropesa Dashboard) with a customized loan offer. You can also use the eligibility page located in your Heropesa Dashboard to gain more insight into the status of your Heropesa Loans eligibility.
What are the potential tax implications of converting sales to bitcoin?
Bitcoin is taxed like any other financial investment, so it’s important to understand the tax implications before you buy or sell it. When you acquire bitcoin on Heropesa, we’ll provide you with a Form 1099-DA for filing your taxes. It’s your responsibility to determine any tax impact of your bitcoin transactions. Heropesa is unable to provide tax advice.
How exactly does the bitcoin conversion feature work?
Choose a percentage of your daily net card sales to automatically convert into bitcoin. We’ll withhold that amount from your sales and automatically convert it into bitcoin every day at the then-current market price. A flat 1% fee will apply to the purchase amount. Selling and sending bitcoin may result in additional fees. See Heropesa Bitcoin Terms for more information.
