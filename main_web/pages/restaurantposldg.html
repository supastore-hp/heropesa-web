FAQ

How does Heropesa work for restaurants?
Heropesa provides a unified POS system that handles ordering, payments, inventory, staff management, and reporting. It's designed to adapt to quick-service, full-service, and bar environments seamlessly.
What's involved in switching from my current restaurant POS solution to Heropesa?
Our onboarding specialists assist with menu migration, hardware setup, and staff training. Most restaurants transition fully within 1-3 days with minimal downtime.
Can I upgrade my plan?
Yes. You can upgrade from Free to Plus or Premium at any time directly from your Heropesa Dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.
Can I downgrade my plan?
Absolutely. Downgrade anytime with no penalties. Your plan adjusts at the start of your next billing cycle.
How long will it take to get started with Heropesa?
You can create an account and process your first payment in under 10 minutes. Full restaurant setup with menus and staff typically takes 1-2 hours.
Does Heropesa work without internet access?
Yes. Offline mode securely stores payment data and automatically processes transactions once connectivity is restored within 24 hours.
What is a restaurant POS system?
A Point of Sale (POS) system is the central hub where orders are entered, payments are processed, inventory is tracked, and business operations are managed.
