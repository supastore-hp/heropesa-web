Comprehensive Fleet Management Features

Everything you need to run your fleet—from real-time tracking to automated compliance reporting—in one integrated suite.

Real-Time GPS Tracking

Live vehicle location tracking with historical route playback, geofencing, and instant arrival/departure notifications.

Predictive Maintenance

Automated service reminders based on engine hours, mileage, or diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs) to prevent costly breakdowns.

Fuel Management

Integrate with fuel cards to track purchases, monitor fuel economy (MPG), and flag suspicious fueling activity instantly.

Driver Behavior Analytics

Scorecard systems that track speeding, harsh cornering, and seatbelt usage, enabling proactive safety management.

Automated Compliance Reporting

Streamline DVIRs, IFTA reporting, and ELD logs with digital forms and automated regulatory submission workflows.

Advanced Route Optimization

AI-powered dispatching that calculates the most efficient multi-stop routes, saving time and reducing mileage.

Asset Utilization Tracking

Monitor the usage rates of trailers, containers, and specialized equipment to maximize ROI and prevent loss.

Mobile Driver App

Empower drivers with a dedicated app for electronic logging, digital inspections, navigation, and two-way messaging.

Vendor & Repair Management

Manage approved repair shops, track warranty claims, and control maintenance spending across your entire network.
