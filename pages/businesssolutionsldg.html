Business management

Sell anywhere and get paid anytime with flexible omnichannel tools that connect and customize all parts of your business.

Invoices & Websites

  • Contract builder: Build and customize professional contracts for your business.
  • Invoicing software: Quickly send and track invoices to save time and get paid faster.
  • ECommerce platform: Turn any business into an online business with a free website.
  • Online ordering: Accept online orders, promote repeat purchases, and streamline deliveries.
  • QR code ordering: Provide contactless, self-serve ordering for your dine-in customers.

Payments & Point of Sale

  • Instant transfers: Get your funds instantly and stay in control of your cash flow.
  • Payment links: Accept payments almost anywhere. Easy to create, share, and track.
  • Heropesa Dashboard: Real-time reports and insights into what’s selling.

APIs, SDKs, and integration partners

Heropesa provides the partners, the platforms, and the tools you need to run your business.

Core Business APIs

  • Bookings API: Integrate Appointments into your application.
  • Catalog API: Programmatically catalog products, services, variations, and taxes.
  • Inventory API: Implement programmatic management of inventory counts.
  • Orders API: Track and manage the lifecycle of a purchase.

Payment & Checkout SDKs

  • In-App Payments SDK: Accept payments within native mobile apps (Android, iOS, Flutter).
  • Web Payments SDK: Build a custom payment flow into a website (Credit card, ACH, digital wallets).
  • Terminal API: Take payments using a Heropesa Terminal from any web/mobile app.
  • Point of Sale API: Take payments using Heropesa Reader or Stand from a native UI.

FAQ

What is business software?
Business software consists of integrated tools designed to help manage operations, process payments, engage customers, and oversee team logistics efficiently from a unified platform.
Can business software help with tasks such as accounting, inventory management, or CRM?
Yes. Heropesa offers built-in inventory management, a robust Customer Directory/CRM, and seamless integrations for your accounting needs.
Which factors should be considered when selecting the right business software?
Consider scalability, ease of use, integration capabilities (APIs/SDKs), hardware compatibility, transparent pricing, and specific tools required for your industry (e.g., KDS for restaurants or Appointments for salons).
Does Heropesa work with popular accounting software, such as XERO and Quickbooks?
Absolutely. Through the Heropesa App Marketplace, you can seamlessly connect your account to popular accounting platforms like Xero and QuickBooks.
Which types of payments can my business accept with Heropesa?
Heropesa accepts all major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, ACH bank transfers, Cash App Pay, and Buy Now, Pay Later options like Afterpay.
For which types of businesses is Heropesa best suited?
Heropesa is built for businesses of all sizes and industries, including retail, restaurants, beauty and personal care, professional services, health and fitness, and home repair.
How secure is the Heropesa payment processing system?
Heropesa provides end-to-end encryption, fraud protection, and a dedicated Payment Risk Manager to detect and manage risk with no extra monthly fees.
How do I get started with Heropesa?
Simply sign up online, choose the hardware or software solutions that fit your business, and start accepting payments and managing your operations immediately.
