Challenges Heropesa Fuel Station Management Solves

Overcome the most common obstacles facing modern fuel retail operations with intelligent, integrated solutions.

Fuel Theft & Syphoning

Detect fraud by capturing actual fuel volume filled vs. receipt and protect against all methods of fuel syphoning, including via return pipe.

Manual Billing Errors

Eliminate mistakes in nozzle reading, rate history, and credit billing with automated, error-proof billing software.

Lack of Real-Time Visibility

Gain instant, centralized visibility into forecourt sales, tank levels, and financial records from any internet-enabled device.

Inventory Shrinkage

Monitor tank-wise stock, purchase rates, and sale rates to prevent waste and ensure optimal product availability.

Fleet Fuel Fraud

Discourage drivers from freewheeling and fuel theft with digital fuel monitoring sensors and GPRS tracking.

Complex Credit Management

Streamline credit billing, statement generation, and payment receipts with a personalised portal for credit customers.
