FAQ

How does buy now, pay later work?
Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL) allows your customers to purchase items immediately and pay for them in installments over a set period. With Afterpay, customers pay in 4 interest-free installments over 6 weeks, while you get paid the full amount upfront.
How do I set up buy now, pay later?
You can enable Afterpay directly from your Heropesa Dashboard under the Payment Methods section. Simply toggle it on to start offering BNPL to your customers across supported Heropesa products.
How much does buy now, pay later cost?
There are no monthly fees or startup costs to enable Afterpay. You only pay a processing fee when a customer successfully checks out using the Afterpay payment method.
What about fraud and chargebacks on BNPL payments?
Afterpay assumes the risk for fraud and chargebacks associated with customer repayment. You are protected from customer payment defaults once the transaction is completed.
What if I don't want to offer Afterpay to my buyers?
Offering Afterpay is entirely optional. You can easily toggle the payment method off in your Heropesa Dashboard settings at any time.
Do my custom processing rates carry over to Afterpay payments as well?
Afterpay transactions typically carry their own specific processing rate distinct from standard credit card processing rates. Please refer to your pricing plan details for the exact rates applied to BNPL transactions.
How can my customers set up Afterpay?
Customers can select Afterpay at checkout. If they are new to Afterpay, they will be prompted to create an account and receive instant approval decisions before completing the purchase.
How do refunds work with Afterpay?
You can process refunds for Afterpay transactions directly through your Heropesa POS or Dashboard, just like a standard card payment. Afterpay will automatically adjust the customer's payment schedule and refund any installments already paid.
