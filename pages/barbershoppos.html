FAQ

How do I switch from my current retail POS solution?
Our onboarding specialists assist with product catalog migration, hardware setup, and staff training. Most retailers transition fully within 1-2 days with minimal downtime.
Can I upgrade my plan?
Yes. You can upgrade from Free to Plus or Premium at any time directly from your Heropesa Dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.
Can I downgrade my plan?
Absolutely. Downgrade anytime with no penalties. Your plan adjusts at the start of your next billing cycle.
What is a retail POS system?
A retail POS is a point-of-sale system that handles transactions, inventory management, customer profiles, reporting, and omnichannel sales in one unified platform.
