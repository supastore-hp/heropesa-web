FAQ

What types of small businesses use Heropesa?
Heropesa serves a wide range of small businesses including retail, food & beverage, professional services, beauty & wellness, home services, and more.
How do Heropesa's tools work together?
All Heropesa products share a unified dashboard. Sales, inventory, payroll, and analytics sync automatically in real time for seamless business management.
What kinds of payment solutions does Heropesa offer?
Heropesa offers in-person POS, online payments, invoicing, virtual terminal, recurring billing, and contactless/NFC payments including Apple Pay and Google Pay.
How does Heropesa save small business owners time?
By combining payments, scheduling, invoicing, customer management, and analytics into one platform, Heropesa eliminates manual admin work and streamlines operations.
Does Heropesa offer CRM or Marketing tools?
Yes. Heropesa includes Customer Directory for contact management, Heropesa Messages for client communication, and marketing tools for loyalty programs and campaigns.
