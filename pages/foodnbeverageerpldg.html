What is Heropesa Food & Beverage ERP?

Heropesa Food & Beverage ERP connects your accounting, lot-level inventory, production, quality, and multichannel order data in a single cloud database with embedded AI functionality. Every PO, batch ticket, and shipment posts once, updates everywhere, and is audit-ready—slashing hours of data-chasing and accelerating business decisions that drive growth and profitability.

Industry-specific tools for product freshness, inventory traceability, and variable weight pricing come built into the core system. The result: key processes from across the business reside in one cloud for real-time visibility into true costs, yields, and every customer commitment.

  • Automated catch-weight & lot-level COGS tracking
  • FEFO planning to minimize spoilage & waste
  • Single-click recall traceability across plants & 3PLs
  • Real-time commodity pricing & margin analytics

Challenges Heropesa Food & Beverage ERP Solves
