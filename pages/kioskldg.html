FAQ

What is Heropesa Kiosk?
Heropesa Kiosk is a self-service ordering and payment solution built for iPad. It integrates directly with your Heropesa POS to streamline front-of-house operations, reduce labor costs, and increase average order value through intuitive upselling.
How much does Heropesa Kiosk cost and what does it include?
The software costs $50/mo per device on Heropesa Plus or $30/mo per device on Heropesa Premium. It includes the Kiosk app, integrated payment processing, menu syncing, loyalty enrollment, and real-time sales reporting.
Do I need to buy special hardware?
Yes. Heropesa Kiosk requires an iPad and the dedicated Heropesa Kiosk Stand (which includes an integrated card reader and receipt printer). The hardware is sold separately but optimized for seamless plug-and-play setup.
What if I need multiple kiosks?
You can deploy as many kiosks as your location requires. Each additional device requires its own monthly software subscription. All kiosks sync to a single Heropesa Dashboard for centralized management.
How do I get started with Heropesa Kiosk?
Purchase the hardware and subscribe to Heropesa Plus or Premium. Once delivered, simply mount your iPad, download the Heropesa Kiosk app, sign in, and your menu will auto-sync from your Heropesa POS.
I have a loyalty program. Can I integrate my loyalty program with the Heropesa Kiosk app?
Yes. Heropesa Kiosk natively supports Heropesa Loyalty, allowing customers to enroll, scan phone numbers, and earn/redeem points directly during self-service checkout.
