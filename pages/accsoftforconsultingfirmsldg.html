What is Heropesa Accounting for Consulting Firms?

Heropesa Accounting Software is a cloud-based financial management solution that integrates core accounting data and processes with CRM, HR, and professional services automation (PSA) systems. It provides consulting firms with complete visibility into cash flows, margins, resource utilization, and compliance status.

By replacing back-office spreadsheets and point solutions with real-time data, Heropesa creates a unified set of records shared by managers, partners, and finance teams—leading to better business alignment and performance.

  • Seamless integration with PSA and CRM systems
  • Real-time visibility into WIP and project margins
  • Automated revenue recognition and billing
  • Elimination of manual data rekeying
