What is Heropesa Nonprofit ERP?

To achieve their goals, nonprofits need clear visibility into finances, programs, and stakeholder relationships. From accounting and inventory management to program oversight and donor engagement, Heropesa ERP gathers daily operational activities in a central database, providing timely, transparent, and actionable insights.

  • GAAP- & FASB-compliant fund accounting
  • Real-time tracking of grants, donations & restrictions
  • Integrated program management & outcome reporting
  • Donor CRM & automated acknowledgment workflows
Nonprofit Operations Workflow Placeholder

Integrated Tools for Every Mission

Replace fragmented tools with a single system that connects the people, processes, and resources behind every initiative.

Accounting Software

Record donations, grants, and program spending securely. Customizable segmentation maintains precise ledgers and tracks donor restrictions effortlessly.

Financial Management

Budgeting, forecasting, and financial close automation tailored for nonprofit fiscal years, multi-fund tracking, and board reporting.

Global Business Management

Manage multi-entity operations, international chapters, and cross-border funding with consolidated reporting and local compliance.

Inventory Management

Track supplies, relief goods, and program materials in real time. Prevent stockouts and reduce waste with automated reorder alerts.

Order & Procurement

Streamline purchase requests, vendor approvals, and PO generation. Maintain audit trails for every dollar spent on programs or operations.

Warehouse Management

Optimize storage, picking, and distribution of donated goods or program supplies across multiple locations.

Supply Chain Management

Gain end-to-end visibility into supplier performance, logistics costs, and delivery schedules for mission-critical operations.

Constituent Relationship Management (CRM)

Manage donors, volunteers, grantors, and beneficiaries in one place. Track engagement history, preferences, and communication touchpoints.
