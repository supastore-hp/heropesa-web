FAQ

How do I get started?
Sign up for a Heropesa account, navigate to the Marketing tab in your Dashboard, select Email or Text Message Marketing, import your customer lists from Heropesa Customer Directory, choose a template, and launch your first campaign in minutes.
Do I need to be subscribed to Heropesa Plus or Heropesa Premium to use email or text message marketing?
Heropesa Email Marketing is available on all plans with a monthly recipient limit. Text Message Marketing requires a paid add-on or is included in higher-tier plans depending on your volume and region.
Can I used Heropesa to post on social media?
Heropesa integrates with platforms like Instagram and Facebook to sync your product catalog and promotions. While it doesn't natively schedule posts, it seamlessly connects your sales and marketing efforts across social channels.
Are automations included in my subscription cost?
Yes. Core automated workflows (welcome, birthday, abandoned cart, win-back) are built into Heropesa Marketing and included with your active marketing subscription at no additional setup fee.
Can I schedule and send email and text campaigns at the same time?
Absolutely. You can schedule multi-channel campaigns to send emails and texts simultaneously or stagger them strategically to maximize open and conversion rates.
Can I create and send coupons with Heropesa?
Yes. Heropesa Marketing includes a built-in coupon creator. You can generate unique promo codes, track redemption rates in real-time, and automatically apply discounts at checkout in-store or online.
Which email marketing tools does Heropesa offer?
Heropesa offers drag-and-drop email builders, pre-designed industry templates, audience segmentation, A/B testing, automated drip campaigns, and detailed performance analytics tied directly to your sales data.
Where can I learn more about text message marketing?
Visit Heropesa's Help Center and Marketing Resource Hub for comprehensive guides, compliance checklists, best-practice templates, and case studies on successful SMS campaigns.
Does Heropesa's marketing tools integrate with other software?
Yes. Heropesa Marketing syncs natively with Heropesa POS, Online Store, Customer Directory, and Loyalty. It also connects to hundreds of third-party apps via the Heropesa App Marketplace for advanced CRM, analytics, and e-commerce workflows.
