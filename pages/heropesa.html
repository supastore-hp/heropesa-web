Questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know about sending money with Heropesa.

What countries can I send to?
You can send money to over 140 countries worldwide, including all of Africa, Europe, Asia, the Americas, and Oceania. We support local bank transfers, mobile wallets (like M-Pesa), and cash pickup options depending on the destination.
How long do transfers take?
Most transfers arrive within seconds to minutes. Some destinations may take up to 1-2 business days depending on local banking systems. You'll always see the estimated arrival time before confirming your transfer.
Are there any hidden fees?
Never. We show all fees upfront before you confirm a transfer. We use the real mid-market exchange rate with no hidden markup. The only cost is our transparent, low fee shown clearly during checkout.
Is my money safe with Heropesa?
Yes. We're regulated in multiple jurisdictions and hold customer funds in segregated accounts with top-tier financial institutions. We use bank-level encryption, 2FA, and 24/7 fraud monitoring to protect your account.
Can I use Heropesa for business?
Absolutely. Heropesa Platform offers APIs, multi-user accounts, invoicing, and compliance tools designed for businesses of all sizes. Contact our sales team for enterprise pricing and custom solutions.
