Fashion Industry Q&A

Answers to common questions from CFOs, Supply Chain Directors, and IT Leaders.

Does Heropesa support size/color matrix inventory?
Yes. Our matrix tools allow you to manage thousands of SKUs based on size, color, and style attributes without data duplication. You can automatically generate SKUs from style templates and perform bulk updates.
Can we manage both wholesale and DTC channels?
Absolutely. Heropesa unifies order, inventory, and customer data across ecommerce, wholesale, and retail. You can manage custom price books, buyer portals, and EDI integrations all in one place.
How does Heropesa handle seasonal demand swings?
Our demand-planning tools forecast sales by SKU, region, and channel to align purchasing and production with actual market needs. You can also use open-to-buy budgeting and markdown planning tools.
What support is included for enterprise clients?
Enterprise clients receive 24/7 priority support, a dedicated Customer Success Manager, quarterly architecture reviews, and access to our Premium Engineering Portal with guaranteed response SLAs under 15 minutes.
Do you offer custom development or white-label solutions?
Yes. We offer full white-label deployments, custom module development, and industry-specific configurations. Our Professional Services team works directly with your IT department to align with your roadmap.
