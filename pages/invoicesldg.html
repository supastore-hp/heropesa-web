FAQ

How can I send my customers an invoice?
You can create and send digital invoices instantly via email, SMS, or a shareable link directly from your Heropesa Dashboard or POS app.
What methods of payment can my customers use?
Customers can pay via credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Cash App Pay, ACH bank transfers, and Afterpay.
How quickly can I access my funds?
Funds are typically available the next business day for free, or instantly for a small fee. Heropesa Checking accounts provide immediate access to sales.
How can I automate my billing system?
Set up recurring invoices, automatic payment reminders, and flexible payment schedules to streamline your cash flow without manual intervention.
Can I send payment receipts?
Yes. Digital receipts are automatically generated and sent to customers upon successful payment.
Does your invoicing software offer integration to QuickBooks Online or other accounting software?
Yes. Heropesa integrates seamlessly with QuickBooks Online, Xero, and other major accounting platforms to keep your books balanced.
Does Heropesa Support multi-currency?
Heropesa Supports multiple currencies depending on your region. Check your local Heropesa Dashboard for specific currency availability.
How can I manage my customers better?
Use the Heropesa Customer Directory to save contact info, payment methods, and purchase history, allowing for personalized service and faster checkouts.
What is invoicing?
Invoicing is the process of sending a detailed bill to a customer for goods or services provided, specifying payment terms and methods.
