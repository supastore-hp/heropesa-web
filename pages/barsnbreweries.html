FAQ

What is a bar POS system?
A bar POS is a point-of-sale system optimized for hospitality venues. It handles drink tabs, inventory tracking for kegs/bottles, split checks, tip management, and integrates with kitchen displays and third-party delivery apps.
What’s the difference between a bar POS and a restaurant POS?
While both process orders and payments, a bar POS emphasizes open tab management, quick pour tracking, drink modifiers, auto-gratuity, and high-speed checkout during peak hours. Restaurant POS systems focus more on table mapping, multi-course ordering, and complex kitchen routing.
What hardware do you need for a bar POS system?
Most bars start with a Heropesa Register or Stand at the main counter, plus Heropesa Handhelds or Terminals for tableside ordering. A KDS screen in the prep area and a receipt printer are highly recommended for smooth operations.
