FAQ

How do I choose a salon software?
Look for software that combines scheduling, payments, client management, and marketing in one platform. Heropesa Appointments offers all these features with no long-term contracts.
Why should professional stylists use the Heropesa hair salon solution?
Heropesa helps stylists save time with automated booking, reduce no-shows with prepayments, manage client preferences, and accept payments anywhere — all from one intuitive system.
Can I add Heropesa Appointments to my salon booking website?
Yes. You can embed Heropesa Appointments booking widgets directly into your existing website, or use our free customizable booking site.
How long does it take to set up Heropesa for my hair salon?
You can be up and running in minutes. Add your services, staff, and availability, then start accepting bookings immediately. Our team can help migrate your existing data if needed.
