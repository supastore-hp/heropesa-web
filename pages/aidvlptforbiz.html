FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions. Find some common questions below about Heropesa

How do we approach a project?
We begin with a Proof of Concept (POC) approach to explore possibilities and craft the most effective AI solution. Our process emphasizes using the right tools for the job, ensuring tailored, impactful outcomes for each client.
What technologies do you specialize in?
We specialize in machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, generative AI, data analytics, and full-stack AI software development. We stay current with emerging frameworks and select the optimal stack for each project.
Can you provide examples of previous work?
Yes. We share case studies and anonymized project summaries upon request. Due to confidentiality agreements, some details may be restricted, but we're happy to discuss relevant experience during initial consultations.
What is your pricing model?
We offer flexible engagement models: fixed-price for well-scoped projects, time-and-materials for iterative development, and retainer agreements for ongoing support. We provide transparent estimates after initial discovery.
How do you ensure the quality and security of the software?
We follow rigorous QA processes including code reviews, automated testing, and security audits. All projects adhere to industry best practices for data encryption, access control, and compliance with relevant regulations (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.).
