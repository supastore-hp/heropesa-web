What is Heropesa Apparel Accounting Software?

Heropsa Apparel Accounting software combines core financial management tools with inventory tracking, supply chain data, and sales performance metrics. Everything runs on one centralized cloud platform, with each transaction—from vendor invoices and POs to customer returns and markdowns—automatically flowing into your general ledger and financial reports.

Disconnected systems are so out of style. With Heropsa, apparel businesses can eliminate point solutions, automate accounting processes, and gain real-time visibility into margins, cash flow, and performance by store, channel, and product line.

  • Automated GL posting from POS, ecommerce & wholesale
  • Dimensional tagging by SKU, season, brand & region
  • Real-time margin & markdown tracking
  • Multi-entity consolidation & intercompany eliminations

Comprehensive Financial Features

Heropsa links financials with inventory, sourcing, production, and fulfillment data so apparel businesses can trim costs, optimize margins, and zip through the financial close.

General Ledger

Flexible GL with dimensional tagging by SKU, brand, season, region, or sales channel. Generates detailed financial and style-level profitability reports without spreadsheets.

Cash Management

Monitor bank balances, reconcile accounts, and manage liquidity across multiple entities and currencies in real time.

Accounts Receivable

Automate invoicing, track aging, and accelerate collections with integrated payment gateways and customer portals.

Accounts Payable

Streamline bill processing, automate approvals, and manage vendor payments efficiently while capturing early-payment discounts.

Account Reconciliation

Automate matching and reconciliation processes to reduce errors, speed up month-end close, and ensure audit readiness.

Tax Management

Calculate sales tax, VAT, and GST automatically based on location and transaction type. Stay compliant with changing regulations across jurisdictions.

Close Management

Orchestrate the financial close process with task lists, automated journals, and real-time visibility into close status across entities.

Fixed Assets Management

Track retail fixtures, equipment, and property assets. Automate depreciation schedules and maintenance alerts to protect capital investments.

Payment Management

Accept and process payments via credit card, ACH, wire, and digital wallets directly within the accounting workflow.
