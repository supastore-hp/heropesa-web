FAQ

Can I use Heropesa Appointments to power my entire spa?
Yes. Heropesa Appointments handles scheduling, payments, client management, inventory tracking, marketing, and reporting — all from one unified platform designed for spa businesses.
What payment methods are supported by Heropesa POS?
Heropesa accepts all major payment types: tap, chip, swipe, card on file, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Cash App Pay, Afterpay, and more — even when offline.
How does Heropesa manage tipping for my spa business?
Heropesa Supports customizable tip prompts at checkout, tip pooling, and automatic tip distribution to staff. All tips are tracked and reported in your dashboard.
Can I book appointments and collect payments from my website?
Yes. You can embed Heropesa Appointments booking widgets directly into your website or use our free customizable booking site. Payments are processed securely in real time.
